The Answers

Answers, before you ask

Most single-service builds go live in 1–4 weeks. Larger combinations (chatbot + CRM + workflows) typically take 4–6 weeks, and full custom software 4–12 weeks. You'll get an exact timeline in your blueprint within 48 hours of our first call.

Every package is custom-scoped to your business, because a 3-person clinic and a 50-person agency need very different systems. After a free 30-minute audit call you'll receive a fixed-price proposal — no hourly billing surprises, no hidden fees.

That's exactly who we build for. Every system ships with plain-language documentation, recorded training for your team, and post-launch support. If you can use WhatsApp, you can run the systems we build.

Yes. We're based in Chandigarh, India and work with clients globally. All collaboration happens over WhatsApp, email and video calls, and our systems are built on cloud platforms that work anywhere in the world.

We're tool-agnostic and pick what fits your budget and scale: n8n, Make and Zapier for workflows; OpenAI and Claude for AI; HubSpot, Zoho and Salesforce for CRM; WhatsApp Cloud API and Twilio for messaging; React and Node.js for custom software. You always own your accounts and your data.

Every package includes post-launch support (14–90 days depending on tier). After that, our Managed Care Plans cover monitoring, error recovery, optimization and new automation each month — or we hand everything over with documentation if you prefer to self-manage.
